Professional Trading System

1. Market Preparation (Before Market Opens)

Check overall market direction.

 ✔ Identify trend on higher timeframe
 ✔ Mark support & resistance levels
 ✔ Identify key zones of liquidity
 ✔ Select 2–5 best stocks / instruments only

Avoid trading too many charts.

2. Timeframe Setup

Primary timeframe:

15 Minute Chart → Main trading decisions

Supporting timeframes:

5 Minute → Entry confirmation
 1 Minute → Price action clarity

Never rely on only one timeframe.

3. Market Structure Check

Confirm structure before entering.

Uptrend:
✔ Higher Highs
✔ Higher Lows

Downtrend:
✔ Lower Highs
✔ Lower Lows

Trade in the direction of the trend whenever possible.

4. Location (Where Price Is)

Only trade at important locations.

Good locations:

✔ Support
✔ Resistance
✔ Breakout level
✔ Retest of breakout
✔ Consolidation zone

Bad locations:

✘ Middle of range
✘ Random candles
✘ No clear structure

5. Entry Confirmation

Enter only when price action confirms.

Valid confirmations:

✔ Breakout with strong momentum
✔ Support bounce
✔ Resistance rejection
✔ Pattern completion

Possible patterns:

• Double top / bottom
• Flags
• Triangles
• M / W patterns

6. Candlestick Confirmation

Look for strong candles:

Bullish
✔ Bullish Engulfing
✔ Hammer
✔ Strong momentum candle

Bearish
✔ Shooting Star
✔ Bearish Engulfing
✔ Strong rejection candle

Reminder:

Pattern without context = weak signal

7. Volume Confirmation

Check participation.

Good signs:

✔ Volume increases on breakout
✔ Volume increases in trend direction
✔ Large volume near key levels

Warning signs:

⚠ Breakout with low volume
⚠ Sudden spike then reversal

8. Risk Management (Mandatory)

Before entering trade:

✔ Stop loss defined
✔ Risk per trade = 1-2% maximum
 ✔ Risk-Reward minimum 1:2

Example:

Risk ₹100
Target ₹200 or ₹300

If RR < 1:2 → skip trade

9. Trade Execution

Once setup is valid:

✔ Enter without hesitation
✔ Follow system rules
✔ Do not change plan mid-trade

Remember:

You will never catch the exact top or bottom.

10. Trade Management

After entry:

✔ Take partial profit at 1:3 RR
 ✔ Move stop loss to break even
 ✔ Trail stop for trend continuation

Goal:

Capture large moves.

11. Daily Risk Rules

Maximum rules per day:

✔ Max 3 stop losses per day
 ✔ After 3 losses → STOP trading

This prevents emotional trading.

12. Psychology Rules

Always remember:

✔ Losses are part of trading
✔ Do not revenge trade
✔ Do not increase position size after losses
✔ Patience beats activity

Rule:

Do not trade just because the market is open.

Trade only when your system gives a signal.

13. Position Size Adjustment

Trend direction matters.

With trend → larger size

Against trend → smaller size

Never average losing trades.

14. After-Trade Routine

After each trade:

✔ Record entry
✔ Record exit
✔ Screenshot chart
✔ Write reason for trade
✔ Note mistakes

This builds your trading edge over time.

15. The Real Goal of a Trader

First milestone:

Become a Break-Even Trader

Most traders fail before reaching this stage.

Once you stop losing consistently:

Profit comes naturally.

The Golden Rule

Successful trading =

Edge + Risk Management + Discipline + Consistency

Not a secret strategy.
